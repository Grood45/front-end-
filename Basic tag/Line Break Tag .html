A cow is a domestic animal.

Cows are one of the most innocent animals who are very harmless. People keep cows at their homes for various benefits.

Cows are four-footed and have a large body.

It has two horns, two eyes plus two ears and one nose and a mouth.

Cows are herbivorous animals.

They have a lot of uses to mankind. In fact, farmers and people keep cows at their homes for the same purposes.
